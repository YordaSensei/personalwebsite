MOVIES

Avengers Endgame

I’m mainly interested in Marvel, but this one made me tear up.
It became even more emotional because of the buildup,
starting from Infinity War (the prequel).

The Nightmare Before Christmas

I really love this movie for its amazing visuals and beautiful music,
which fit the story's mix of whimsy and spookiness.
The detailed stop-motion animation is so impressive,
and it makes me appreciate the effort that goes into creating films like this.

Haikyuu!! The Dumpster Battle

This is an anime movie I like because the fight between the two teams-
had been hyped since the start of the series.
Watching it in theaters was far more exciting than online,
with the loud sound of the ball spike making the attacks feel more intense.
It’s just a bit disappointing that the movie felt so short.

ARTISTS

Rex Orange County

Rex’s songs are mostly iconic to one another,
but it has this appeal because of his unfiltered voice.
I think he sounds way better in live or in concerts.

Tyler, the Creator

He has been one of my favorites just recently.
It’s like he made his own genre in the music industry,
and I admit that most of them are catchy.

Laufey

I grew up loving jazz, but the genre isn’t really famous at this time.
But Laufey really stands out, resurrecting the art of jazz music.

MUSIC

The Way You Look Tonight

Sang by Frank Sinatra, a song that kept playing in my head since grade school.
It’s a lovely song that is catchy as well.

Remember Summer Days

A Japanese song composed by Anri and Night Tempo.
I get more energized when listening to city pop music,
and this is one of the best ones for me.

SWEET / I THOUGHT YOU WANTED TO DANCE

A song made by Tyler, The Creator. It’s a long song,
but it’s basically two parts in one song.
And both of them really fits his vibe.
