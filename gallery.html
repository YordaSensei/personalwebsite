Insights

Honestly, I used to believe I wasn’t capable of creating anything artistic.
However, when we had an art subject, we were tasked with making an artwork.
To pass with a decent grade, I reluctantly gave pixel art a try.
Surprisingly, I found it pretty fun as I kept working on it.
While my pieces weren’t perfect and didn’t follow any art theories, I’m proud of what I created.

Insights

Work Immersion was one of the most memorable experiences of my life. As a programming student,
I chose not to join a tech-related company. Instead, I went to LJQ Multimedia Productions,
a company suited for arts and animation students, because I was more confident in video editing than coding at the time.

Surrounded by talented artists, I felt nervous and worried about being a burden to the team.
However, I decided to face the challenge head-on. While the tasks were numerous and often stressful,
working alongside a supportive team made it worthwhile.
In the end, I’m glad I contributed final outputs to the company. It was a rewarding experience.
